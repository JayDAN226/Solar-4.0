Planets

Mercury

The smallest planet in our solar system and the closest to the sun. Diameter: 4,879 km

Venus

Known for its thick atmosphere and bright appearance in the night sky. Diameter: 12,104 km

Earth

Our home planet, the only known celestial body to support life. Diameter: 12,742 km

Mars

Known as the Red Planet, Mars has distinctive iron oxide surfaces. Diameter: 6,779 km

Jupiter

The largest planet in our solar system, known for its prominent bands of clouds. Diameter: 139,820 km

Saturn

Famous for its stunning ring system composed of ice particles and dust. Diameter: 116,460 km

Uranus

Uranus rotates on its side, and it has a pale blue-green color due to methane in its atmosphere. Diameter: 50,724 km

Neptune

The farthest known planet in our solar system, with a deep blue color and strong winds. Diameter: 49,244 km
